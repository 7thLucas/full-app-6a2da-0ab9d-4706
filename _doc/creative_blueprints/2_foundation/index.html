Foundation Validated

Fragmented property management and client tracking

The Problem

Real estate agents spend too much time managing spreadsheets, coordinating viewings across multiple channels, and manually matching buyers to properties. Critical deals are delayed or lost due to poor organization and slow response times.

The Solution

Real estate agents juggle multiple spreadsheets, emails, and phone calls to manage listings, coordinate viewings, and track offers. Without a centralized system, properties sit longer on the market, buyer-agent matches are inefficient, and deals slip through the cracks due to poor communication and lost follow-ups.

Target Audience

Individual agents and small to mid-sized brokerages managing 10-100+ active listings

Personal Motivation

Help agents reclaim time from administrative tasks and focus on relationship-building and closing deals, while reducing the time properties spend on the market.
