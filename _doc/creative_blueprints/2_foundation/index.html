The Problem & The Solution

The Problem

Deals Are Lost in the Chaos

Listings, buyer preferences, and viewings are scattered across emails, spreadsheets, and memory — nothing is in one place

Matching the right buyer to the right property happens too slowly — opportunities go to faster competitors while agents are still cross-referencing

Offer tracking is manual and fragmented — negotiation momentum stalls, follow-ups are missed, and deals fall through the cracks

Every slow match and missed follow-up is revenue walking out the door

The Solution

One Hub. Every Deal.

Centralise all property listings with live status, pricing, and full details — everything on the agent's books in one register

Store buyer profiles with preferences and budget, then instantly surface matching properties without manual cross-referencing

Log viewings and track every offer stage — see the full pipeline at a glance and act before deal momentum stalls

Built for agents who close, not agents who chase paperwork
