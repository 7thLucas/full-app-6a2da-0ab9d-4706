TBD — to be confirmed during onboarding

## What It Is
A purpose-built operational app for real estate agents to manage property listings, track client viewings, log offers, and match buyers to the right properties — so agents close deals faster and lose fewer opportunities to slower competitors.

## Primary Users
Real estate agents — individual agents and small agencies who actively manage property listings, client relationships, and deal pipelines.

## Core Problem
Real estate agents juggle listings, buyer preferences, viewing schedules, and offer timelines across scattered tools — spreadsheets, notes, and emails. Deals slip because:
- Matching the right buyer to the right property happens too slowly, giving competitors the edge
- Client viewings go untracked and post-viewing feedback is lost
- Offer stages are managed manually, causing negotiation momentum to stall and deals to fall through

## Solution
A single workspace where agents can:
1. Add and manage property listings with status (available, under offer, sold), pricing, and full details
2. Maintain buyer profiles capturing preferences, budget range, and requirements
3. Schedule and track client viewings with attendance records and feedback logs
4. Log, update, and monitor offers through every negotiation stage
5. Match buyers to suitable properties based on their stored criteria — surfacing the right listings instantly

## Core Features

### 1. Property Listing Manager
Add, edit, and track listings with live status, pricing, photos, and full property details. A central register of everything on the agent's books.

### 2. Client & Buyer Profiles
Capture and store buyer preferences, budget range, location criteria, and requirements so every buyer's needs are known and searchable.

### 3. Viewing Tracker
Schedule viewings, log attendance, record post-viewing feedback, and track which buyers have seen which properties.

### 4. Offer Pipeline
Record all offers against a property, track negotiation stages (submitted → negotiating → accepted / rejected / withdrawn), and maintain a clear view of deal progress.

### 5. Buyer–Property Matching
Surface suitable listings for each buyer based on their stored criteria — eliminating manual cross-referencing and accelerating the path from inquiry to viewing to offer.

## Strategic Goal
Close deals faster by reducing the time between buyer identification and offer acceptance — through systematic buyer–property matching, clear pipeline visibility, and proactive offer and viewing tracking.

## Brand Tone
Professional, precise, results-oriented. Built for serious agents who measure success by deals closed, not features used.

## Positioning
An operational CRM purpose-built for real estate agents — not a generic CRM adapted for property, but a deal-closing tool designed around how agents actually work: listings in, buyers in, match them, schedule viewings, track offers, close.

## Key Value Metrics
- Time saved on manual coordination and cross-referencing
- Reduction in missed follow-ups and stalled offer negotiations
- Increase in buyer–property match rate (viewings booked per buyer)
- Deal velocity: days from first contact to offer accepted
